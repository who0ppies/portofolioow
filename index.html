About Me

Hi!

My name is Fadiyah Syafiqah, but you can call me Fikah. I’m a student from SMK Taruna Bhakti, and I aspire to be a UI/UX Designer. I’m deeply passionate about creativity, details, and how design can shape people’s experiences. For me, UI/UX is more than just visuals — it’s about understanding emotions, solving problems, and creating something meaningful. I want to design digital spaces that are not only functional but also calm, elegant, and full of character.
